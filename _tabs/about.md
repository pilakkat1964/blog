## About Me

I'm **Santhosh Kumar Pilakkat**, a Senior Systems Architect based in Singapore with 30+ years of experience delivering mission-critical systems across telecommunications, defence-grade SDR platforms, and national-scale R&D programs.

Most recently I was a **Senior Principal Research Engineer** at [A\*STAR's Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R)](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/i2r) (July 2000 – March 2025), where I led architecture for industrial 5G, Software-Defined Radio, IIoT, and secure communications programs.

## What I Write About

This blog documents my technical explorations across the following areas:

- **5G & Wireless** — Private 5G, TSN over 5G, LTE, OFDM, adaptive modulation
- **Software-Defined Radio (SDR)** — FPGA-based modem design, JTRS SCA, waveform development
- **IIoT & Smart Manufacturing** — RAMI 4.0, IIC IIRA, industrial connectivity
- **FPGA & Embedded Systems** — Xilinx Zynq/UltraScale/Virtex, VHDL/Verilog/SystemC, PCIe, AXI
- **Systems Architecture & MBSE** — SysML/UML, IBM Rhapsody, model-driven development
- **Software & OS** — Embedded Linux, RTOS (VxWorks, FreeRTOS), C/C++, Python, Rust
- **DIY Electronics & Software** — Personal projects at the intersection of hardware and software
